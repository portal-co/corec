# The Fog

## Context

In recent events, child safety has become a huge priority. However, most systems and laws for child safety concentrate on censorship, which opens loopholes for abuse and oppression. However, these regulations have other, compliant (or unpunishable non-compliant) solutions: encryption.

## Main Idea

The Fog intends to impose a "fog" of anonymity on everyone. While reach turns to zero, the core idea of the internet--decentralization and connectedness--stays strong, in contrast to censorship, which destroys both ideals.

## Mechanics

- Infrastructure and Software: isolated infrastructure like cloud tooling, software downloads, AI and other isolated services, and minimal chat apps trivially have this fog--and, since users are identified by keys here--we spoil any tracking they may do.
- mTLS: user services can be allowed simply by authenticating with mTLS, regardless of content--the protection lies in certificate distribution, not content restriction
